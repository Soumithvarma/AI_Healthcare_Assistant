Disease: Bronchitis

Category: Respiratory

Overview:
Bronchitis is a medical condition that may affect overall health and daily functioning. Early recognition and appropriate medical care are important.

Common Symptoms:
- Fatigue
- Pain or discomfort
- Fever or weakness
- Reduced daily activity tolerance
- Condition-specific symptoms

Emergency Symptoms:
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe pain
- Confusion
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe dehydration

Risk Level Indicators:

Low Risk:
- Mild symptoms
- Normal eating and drinking
- Stable condition

Moderate Risk:
- Persistent symptoms
- Weakness or discomfort
- Reduced appetite or activity

High Risk:
- Severe symptoms
- Organ complications
- Emergency warning signs

Precautions:
- Maintain proper hygiene
- Stay hydrated
- Take adequate rest
- Follow healthy lifestyle habits
- Avoid known triggers

Treatment Guidance:
- Follow doctor-recommended treatment
- Monitor symptoms regularly
- Maintain hydration and nutrition
- Seek medical care if symptoms worsen

When to Consult a Doctor:
- Symptoms persist or worsen
- Emergency symptoms appear
- Daily activities become difficult
- Severe weakness develops

Prevention:
- Maintain healthy lifestyle habits
- Practice hygiene and sanitation
- Attend regular medical checkups
- Follow preventive healthcare advice
