Symptom: Fatigue

Category: General Symptom Knowledge

Overview:
Fatigue is a common symptom that may occur in multiple medical conditions. Severity can range from mild to serious depending on associated symptoms and duration.

Possible Causes:
- Viral infections
- Bacterial infections
- Allergies
- Lifestyle factors
- Chronic medical conditions
- Environmental triggers

Common Associated Symptoms:
- Weakness
- Fatigue
- Pain or discomfort
- Fever
- Reduced daily activity

Emergency Warning Signs:
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe pain
- Loss of consciousness
- Confusion
- Severe dehydration

Self-Care Guidance:
- Take adequate rest
- Maintain hydration
- Eat nutritious food
- Monitor symptoms regularly
- Avoid known triggers if possible

When to Consult a Doctor:
- Symptoms persist for several days
- Symptoms become severe
- Emergency warning signs appear
- Daily activities become difficult

Prevention:
- Maintain good hygiene
- Follow healthy lifestyle habits
- Get adequate sleep
- Stay hydrated
- Seek early medical advice when needed
